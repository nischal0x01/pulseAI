# Team Photos

Place team member photos in this directory with the following naming convention:

## Expected Filenames:

- `rabindra-bista.jpg` - Supervisor
- `suvesh-gurung.jpg` - Team Member
- `pramisha-sapkota.jpg` - Team Member
- `nischal-subedi.jpg` - Team Member
- `arwin-shrestha.jpg` - Team Member

## Supported Formats:

- `.jpg` or `.jpeg`
- `.png`
- `.webp`

## Recommended Specifications:

- **Size**: 400x400px or larger (square format)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Quality**: High quality, well-lit photos
- **Background**: Professional background preferred

Note: If an image is not found, the card will automatically fallback to displaying the member's initials with a gradient background.
